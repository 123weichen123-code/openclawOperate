# 2026 F1 全年赛道安排与赛道特性总览

> 数据来源以 Formula1.com 官方赛历与各分站官方页面为主；截至 2026-03-23，赛季已完成澳大利亚站、中国站，下一站为日本铃鹿。

## 一、全年关键节点

- 02-11 ~ 02-13｜Pre-season Testing 1｜官方页：https://www.formula1.com/en/racing/2026/pre-season-testing-1
- 02-18 ~ 02-20｜Pre-season Testing 2｜官方页：https://www.formula1.com/en/racing/2026/pre-season-testing-2
- 03-06 ~ 03-29｜开季三连：澳大利亚 -> 中国 -> 日本。适合尽快判断新规则下各车队真实实力。
- 05-22 ~ 06-14｜北美/欧洲切换段：加拿大 -> 摩纳哥 -> 巴塞罗那。非常适合观察升级包与不同类型赛道适应度。
- 06-26 ~ 07-26｜欧洲高强度夏季段：奥地利 -> 英国 -> 比利时 -> 匈牙利。高速、传统、技术型赛道连续出现。
- 07-27 ~ 08-20｜夏休窗口。适合回看上半赛季趋势、升级包成效、积分形势。
- 09-04 ~ 09-26｜三站连续：蒙扎 -> 马德里 -> 巴库。低阻、高速、新赛道、街道赛混合，信息量极大。
- 10-23 ~ 12-06｜美洲/中东收官六连段：COTA -> 墨西哥 -> 巴西 -> 拉斯维加斯 -> 卡塔尔 -> 阿布扎比。年度争冠与车队排名通常在这里定局。
- 2026 特别看点：同一赛季同时有巴塞罗那-加泰罗尼亚与西班牙（马德里）两站西班牙分站。

## 二、阅读这份资料时建议重点看什么

- `赛道长度 + 圈数 + 比赛距离`：帮助快速判断比赛节奏、轮胎窗口、进站策略结构。
- `最快单圈 + 折算平均速度`：平均速度越高，通常越考验气动效率、稳定性和车手信心。
- `赛道特性`：看这站更偏向高速下压力、低速机械抓地、制动稳定性，还是轮胎管理。
- `关键赛事节点`：我额外给你挑出每一站值得重点回看的看点和对应官方页。

## 三、分站详解

### Round 1｜03-06 ~ 03-08｜Australia GP

![](https://media.formula1.com/image/upload/c_fit,h_704/q_auto/v1740000001/common/f1/2026/track/2026trackmelbournedetailed.webp)

- 赛道/场地：Albert Park Circuit
- 官方分站页：https://www.formula1.com/en/racing/2026/australia
- 赛道长度：5.278km
- 正赛圈数：58
- 正赛总里程：306.124km
- 最快单圈纪录：1:19.813｜Charles Leclerc (2024)
- 纪录单圈折算平均速度：238.1 km/h
- 首次举办 F1：1996
- 赛道特性：
  - 半街道属性，周末初期抓地力通常偏低。
  - 高速变向+制动区并存，比较考验前轴响应。
  - 赛季揭幕战，车队真实节奏容易在这里第一次被看清。
- 官方描述摘录：As a temporary facility, Albert Park can be quite bumpy, while the circuit at the start of the weekend is often slippery, rubbering in as the sessions progress. It’s also a circuit that requires a well-sorted chassis, with several spots on the track where the drivers require a reactive front end to allow them to chuck it into a corner.
- 观赛/关注点：If you want to get in a grandstand, opt for either the Waite stand overlooking Turn 10, or the Brabham Grandstand on the outside of Turn 2 to watch cars jostling for position through the first chicane. Alternatively, there are some nice general admission spots on the far side of the lake – go for either between Turns 8-9 or 9-10 if you fancy some F1 picnic feels.
- 关键赛事节点/推荐关注：
  - 已完赛，可先从官方分站页查看结果、回放入口与周末时间线：https://www.formula1.com/en/racing/2026/australia

### Round 2｜03-13 ~ 03-15｜China GP

![](https://media.formula1.com/image/upload/c_fit,h_704/q_auto/v1740000001/common/f1/2026/track/2026trackshanghaidetailed.webp)

- 赛道/场地：Shanghai International Circuit
- 官方分站页：https://www.formula1.com/en/racing/2026/china
- 赛道长度：5.451km
- 正赛圈数：56
- 正赛总里程：305.066km
- 最快单圈纪录：1:32.238｜Michael Schumacher (2004)
- 纪录单圈折算平均速度：212.7 km/h
- 首次举办 F1：2004
- 赛道特性：
  - 超长后直道和发夹弯决定超车窗口。
  - T1-T2-T3 连续盘旋弯对前胎管理要求高。
  - 经常适合作为攻防策略和轮胎退化观察样本。
- 官方描述摘录：The circuit’s pleasing form when seen from the air – it’s designed to look like the Chinese symbol for ‘shang’, meaning upwards – is equally pleasing to the drivers on terra firma. There’s a unique start to the lap as the drivers fly into the ever-tightening Turns 1 and 2, before they dart left through 3 and 4.
- 观赛/关注点：We’re going to recommend Grandstand K, overlooking the Turn 14-15 hairpin, where you’ll be in the best seats to watch all the overtaking. Alternatively, head for Turn 6, the track’s other best passing spot, where Daniel Ricciardo sealed his 2018 win with a brave move on Valtteri Bottas.

### Round 3｜03-27 ~ 03-29｜Japan GP

![](https://media.formula1.com/image/upload/c_fit,h_704/q_auto/v1740000001/common/f1/2026/track/2026tracksuzukadetailed.webp)

- 赛道/场地：Suzuka Circuit
- 官方分站页：https://www.formula1.com/en/racing/2026/japan
- 赛道长度：5.807km
- 正赛圈数：53
- 正赛总里程：307.471km
- 最快单圈纪录：1:30.965｜Kimi Antonelli (2025)
- 纪录单圈折算平均速度：229.8 km/h
- 首次举办 F1：1987
- 赛道特性：
  - 高速 S 弯、Degner、130R 连续考验胆量与节奏。
  - 真正的高下压力高速赛道，排位赛含金量高。
  - 车手普遍很喜欢，失误代价也高。
- 官方描述摘录：Show us a racing a driver who doesn’t love Suzuka, and we’ll show you a liar. The high-speed track remains one of the ultimate driving challenges, with the snaking ‘S’ Curves, the two commitment-rewarding Degners and the white-knuckle ride of 130R all highlights in a series of highlights at what is one of F1’s seminal tracks.
- 观赛/关注点：Take your pick. Grandstands D and E overlook the ‘S’ Curves, giving you an amazing chance to watch downforce and commitment working in perfect harmony as the drivers negotiate the sinuous turns.
- 关键赛事节点/推荐关注：
  - 这是当前最近一站，建议重点跟：练习赛长距离节奏、排位 S1/S2 高速段表现、正赛轮胎衰减。官方页：https://www.formula1.com/en/racing/2026/japan

### Round 6｜06-05 ~ 06-07｜Monaco GP

![](https://media.formula1.com/image/upload/c_fit,h_704/q_auto/v1740000001/common/f1/2026/track/2026trackmontecarlodetailed.webp)

- 正赛圈数：78

- 最快单圈纪录：1:12.909｜Lewis Hamilton (2021)

### Round 10｜07-17 ~ 07-19｜Belgium GP

![](https://media.formula1.com/image/upload/c_fit,h_704/q_auto/v1740000001/common/f1/2026/track/2026trackspafrancorchampsdetailed.webp)

- 赛道/场地：Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps
- 官方分站页：https://www.formula1.com/en/racing/2026/belgium
- 赛道长度：7.004km
- 正赛圈数：44
- 正赛总里程：308.052km
- 最快单圈纪录：1:44.701｜Sergio Perez (2024)
- 纪录单圈折算平均速度：240.8 km/h
- 首次举办 F1：1950
- 赛道特性：
  - 超长圈长，天气和海拔变化经常左右比赛。
  - Eau Rouge/Raidillon、Pouhon 等高速段要求高。

- 官方描述摘录：Spa is among Formula 1 drivers’ most loved tracks, with its mix of long straights and challenging fast corners allowing them to push their cars to the edge of their capabilities – if it’s dry, that is. The size of the track and the nature of Belgian weather means it can sometimes be raining on one part of the track and dry on another, meaning grip can vary from one corner to the next.
- 观赛/关注点：The elevated grandstand located near Eau Rouge gives you the best view of the iconic corner, while also boasting panoramic views from La Source through to Raidillon. But if you fancy taking in some of the best trackside atmosphere, then the grandstand located on the Pouhon corner should be right up your street.

  - 天气变量大，排位和正赛都值得回看。 官方页：https://www.formula1.com/en/racing/2026/belgium
